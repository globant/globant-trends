View More

It might sound simple and not very sophisticated, but sketching is a powerful methodology that is used across the board in all creative disciplines from cinema to visual art, to dancing routines to digital art.
It allows you to visualize your ideas and quickly improve them without having to invest too much time or effort.

It’s not about the quality of the sketch, it’s about the process.
So grab a piece of paper, a pencil (or some crayons if you want) and start sketching your ideas, and you’ll see how everything starts falling in shape!
